Biogeography & Biodiversity Research Group

The beauty of nature stems from simple rules, yet it becomes diverse and complex through the touch of randomness. Our mission is to uncover the essence of these rules and use science to decode the intricate mysteries of ecology.

Who we are?

We are a dynamic and enthusiastic team passionate about all aspects of biogeography, biodiversity, theoretical ecology, and paleobiology, who are:

  • Intellectual Curiosity
  • Interdisciplinary Collaboration
  • Flexibility and Adaptability
  • Commitment to Scientific Integrity
  • Effective Communication Skills
  • Resilience in Scientific Endeavors
  • Inclusivity and Diversity

Maintained by Huijie Qiao and his lab members.
