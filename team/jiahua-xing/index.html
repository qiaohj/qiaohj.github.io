Biography

I joined Biogeography & Biodiversity Research Group in 2021 after receiving my B.S. degree in Biological Technology from Tongji University and obtained my M.S. degree in ecology here in 2024. I’m currently a PhD student focus on urban landscape ecology. I also have experience in fieldworks studying bird breeding ecology, bird survey, bird ringing and tracking.

Research Interests

  • The relationship between landscape structure and its capacity to hold avian biodiversity in both coarse and fine scales.
  • Latitudinal gradient pattern of functional traits of birds.
  • Ecological applications of eBird dataset.
