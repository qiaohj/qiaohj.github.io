Jianxia Liu

Biography

I joined the team in 2024 as an engineer. Prior to joining, I had nearly 20 years of experience in the IT industry, with extensive expertise in software development and team management. I have worked at companies such as Oracle, Amazon, and Baidu, and have accumulated numerous successful project experiences in the fields of big data processing, e-commerce, and artificial intelligence. I hold over 10 domestic and international patents.

Research Interests

I primarily focuses on leveraging artificial intelligence technology to explore, monitor, and conserve biodiversity, including developing algorithms to identify species, analyzing ecosystem data, and predicting changes in biodiversity.
