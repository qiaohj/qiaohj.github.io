Opportunity

We open to sharing ideas, data, and methods with researchers all around the world, and welcome communication and collaboration in various forms.

To be honest, we have limited resources, but continue to strive to create opportunities for young scholars.

If you’re interested, feel free to contact Huijie Qiao directly to discuss related topics. Additionally, CAS offers two programs specifically for non-Chinese researchers (ANSO and PIFI), and we would be happy to assist young scholars in these fields with the applications.

Maintained by Huijie and his lab members.
